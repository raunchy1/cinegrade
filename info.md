# CINEGRADE LAB — Research Findings for Design

## Product Overview
CINEGRADE LAB is a premium platform for 125+ Lightroom presets (.XMP) and 125+ cinematic LUTs (.CUBE), targeting professional photographers, filmmakers, colorists, and content creators. Think "Apple + Netflix + DaVinci Resolve + Leica" aesthetic.

## Key Features to Showcase
1. **Premium Preset Library** — 125 hand-crafted Lightroom presets across 25 categories
2. **Cinematic LUT Collection** — 125 professional 3D LUTs for DaVinci Resolve/Premiere/Final Cut
3. **Live Preview Engine** — Before/after comparison slider
4. **AI Color Assistant** — Recommends presets based on mood, camera, lighting
5. **Film Emulation** — Kodak Portra, Fuji 400H, CineStill 800T, ARRI Alexa, Bleach Bypass
6. **Categories**: Luxury Editorial, Cinematic Shadows, Analog Film, Moody Documentary, Dreamy Wedding, Hyperreal Fashion, Night Neon, Luxury Travel, Minimal Scandinavian, Cyberpunk Cinema, Neo Noir, Vintage Film Lab, High Dynamic Luxury, Dark Instagram Mood, Clean Creator Economy, Music Video Looks, Commercial Luxury, Netflix Inspired, A24 Inspired, Kodak Portra Emulation, Fuji 400H Emulation, ARRI Alexa Look, CineStill 800T Emulation, Bleach Bypass, Teal & Orange Modern
